Elote (Mexican Street Corn)

Description

This will be a paragrah talking about the elote

This will be a paragraph talking about where the recipe comes from and changes I've made

Ingredients

  • 4 ears Sweet Corn
  • 1/4th cup melted Butter
  • 1/4 cup Mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup grated Mexican Cheese
  • 4 wedges of Lime

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat.
  3. Grill corn on the preheated grill until hot and lightly charred all over, 7-10 minutes.
  4. Roll corn in melted butter, then spread evenly with mayonnaise. Sprinkle with mexican cheese.
  5. Serve with lime wedges.
